Yun's Birthday Scavenger Hunt

Rules

  • You do NOT need to get everything on the list.
  • The person with the highest points wins.
  • Beware of the finger cross:
    • Cross your finger if you want someone's picture to be considered nulled.
    • If a person in a photo has his/her finger crossed, the photo is considered nulled.
  • You can BLOCK people from taking pictures but also try not to get YOUR photo taken!!
  • There will be a prize at the end of the Hunt.
  • Good Luck!!!

Points

  • Selfie with the birthday girl
  • Photo of the birthday girl holding her cake
  • Photo of the birthday girl laughing with someone at the same time

  • Selfie with the birthday cake

  • A group photo with the birthday girl (Must include a total of 3 people including the birthday girl)

  • Picture of at least 3 people talking together
  • Picture of someone talking to the birthday girl
  • Picture of the birthday girl eating or drinking something

  • Picture of someone eating something
  • Picture of someone drinking something
  • Picture of someone laughing
  • Selfie of yourself with a balloon
